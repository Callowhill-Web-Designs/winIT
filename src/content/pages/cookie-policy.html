1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to website owners.

2. How We Use Cookies

WinIT Consulting uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience, analyze website traffic, and understand where our visitors are coming from. We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Essential Cookies: These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly
  • Analytics Cookies: These help us understand how visitors interact with our website
  • Functional Cookies: These enable enhanced functionality and personalization
  • Marketing Cookies: These may be used to track visitors across websites

3. Types of Cookies We Use

3.1 Essential Cookies

These cookies are strictly necessary for the operation of our website. They enable basic functions like page navigation and access to secure areas of the website. The website cannot function properly without these cookies.

3.2 Analytics Cookies

We may use analytics cookies to collect information about how visitors use our website. This includes information like the number of visitors, the pages they visit, and where they came from. This information helps us improve our website.

3.3 Functional Cookies

These cookies allow the website to remember choices you make and provide enhanced, more personal features. They may also be used to provide services you have asked for, such as remembering your preferences.

3.4 Marketing Cookies

These cookies may be set through our site by our advertising partners. They may be used to build a profile of your interests and show you relevant adverts on other sites.

4. Third-Party Cookies

We may also use third-party services that set cookies on our website. These may include:

  • Google Analytics for website analytics
  • Social media platforms for social sharing features
  • Customer support chat services
  • Email marketing platforms

5. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You have the right to choose whether or not to accept cookies. You can control and/or delete cookies as you wish. You can:

  • Delete all cookies that are already on your computer
  • Set your browser to prevent cookies from being placed
  • Accept or reject cookies on a case-by-case basis

5.1 Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings preferences. However, if you limit the ability of websites to set cookies, you may worsen your overall user experience.

5.2 Opt-Out Links

For more information about advertising cookies and how to opt out, you can visit:

  • Digital Advertising Alliance Opt-Out
  • Network Advertising Initiative Opt-Out
  • Google Analytics Opt-Out

6. Cookie Consent

By continuing to use our website, you consent to our use of cookies as described in this Cookie Policy. If you do not agree to our use of cookies, you should not use our website or should disable cookies in your browser.
